Arcade!

NL

In this project, I attempted to recreate the Wee thanks game for the Wii. Creating the player movement, bullet bounces and the different enemy AI. There are currently three levels in the game, in which the player only has a singular live. meaning that, if defeated, the player has to restart from the very first level.

I spend most of my time, during this project, to create different enemies that all acted differently. Using a single script that checks the enemy's tag to unlock or lock certain behaviors. So does the grey always try to move towards the player using the NavMesh, and does the yellow one move to a random spot on the map. The bullets fired from anyone, player or AI, will bounce of walls once and can collide with one another. if the bullets hit a tank or bullet, both get destroyed. When bullets hit the wall, they change direction based on how it hit the wall. Also changing direction visualy.
