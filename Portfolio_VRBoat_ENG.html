EXHO VR project

ENG

With this project I was allowed to be a part of the third years and worked together with EXHO. During this project we made a VR simulation of inland shipping, to get people interested in it. I was the one in control of the water gate and made it completly automatic.

The communication with EXHO wasn't as positive as we had hoped, but even so we still managed to make something that looks nice. The communication with the group went pretty well, even though some were extremely focused at times on their part that they needed a tap on the shoulder to start listening.
